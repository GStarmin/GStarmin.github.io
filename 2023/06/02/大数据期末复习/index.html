Supervised learning/Unsupervised learning/Semi-supervised learning

  • Supervised learning: targets to learn the mapping function or relationship between the features and the labels based on the labeled data. Namely, \(ğ‘Œ=ğ¹(ğ‘‹|ğœƒ)\). (e.g. Classification, Prediction)
  • Unsupervised learning: aims at learning the intrinsic structure from unlabeled data. (e.g. Clustering, Latent Factor Learning and Frequent Items Mining)
